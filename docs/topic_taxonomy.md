# Douban Comment Topic Taxonomy

This taxonomy defines the topic labels used for LLM-based classification
of Douban short comments related to Chinese dramas.

## PLOT
Discussion about story structure, pacing, script quality, plot logic,
plot holes, or narrative development.

## ACTING
Discussion about acting performance, line delivery, emotional expression,
and character portrayal.

## CAST
Discussion about actor selection, casting decisions, role suitability,
and actor lineup.

## PRODUCTION
Discussion about cinematography, editing, costumes, props, music,
visual effects, or set design.

## RATING
Discussion about Douban ratings, scoring behavior, rating manipulation,
or star ratings.

## PLATFORM_CAPITAL
Discussion about streaming platforms, marketing strategies, PR,
capital influence, water army, or scheduling.

## INFO
Factual information such as release dates, episode counts,
official announcements, or viewing statistics.

## META_FANDOM
Discussion about fandom conflicts, fan/anti-fan behavior,
comment control, reporting, or group disputes.

## OTHER
Comments that cannot clearly be assigned to any category above.
